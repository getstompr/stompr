# Product Overview

## What we are building
A services-led B2B AI concierge platform for luxury travel agencies. Agencies embed a branded widget on their website. The assistant qualifies inbound travelers, recommends options grounded in agency data, and hands high-intent leads to human advisors.

## ICP
- Luxury leisure agencies.
- Bespoke/high-touch advisor teams.
- Agencies with meaningful inbound website traffic and CRM workflows.

## Core outcomes
- Higher lead capture rate from website visitors.
- Better structured qualification data before advisor contact.
- Faster advisor handoff and reduced response-time friction.

## Why B2B first
- Faster path to revenue than pure B2C marketplace dynamics.
- Existing agencies already own traveler trust and traffic.
- Pilot outcomes can be measured with conversion and booking funnel metrics.

## V1 scope
- Lead capture and qualification.
- RAG-grounded recommendations with source citations.
- CRM handoff payload generation.
- No direct booking execution in V1.

## Delivery model
Hybrid: reusable software core plus consulting-style onboarding/customization. This supports premium pricing and faster trust-building with luxury agencies.
